# Privacy Policy for Tennis Point Logger

**Last Updated:** May 11, 2026

Tennis Point Logger is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data within the application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Tennis Point Logger is designed to help users track tennis scores. 
- **Personal Data:** We do not collect any personal identity information (such as your name, address, or phone number) during the normal use of the app.
- **Google Sign-In:** If you choose to use the Google Sign-In feature, we only use your account information to facilitate data backup to your own Google Drive or our secure cloud storage.

## 2. Technical Data & Analytics
To ensure the app is stable and bug-free, we use **Firebase Crashlytics**.
- This service collects technical information such as device model, operating system version, and crash reports.
- This data is used exclusively for debugging and improving app performance. It does not identify you personally.

## 3. Data Storage
Your match data and scores are stored **locally on your device**. 
- If you use the backup or export features (CSV/Cloud), your data is handled via the specific services you authorize (e.g., Google Drive, email sharing).

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Play Services**
- **Firebase Crashlytics**
- **Google Sign-In**

Each of these services has its own privacy policy which you can find on their respective websites.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Tennis Point Logger does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
